1. abstract

The Read-Shockley model was a widely accepted model for interpreting the energy dependence of small-angle grain boundaries. In this study, we report `the collapse model`, which was found in the energy dependence of high-angle symmetric twist grain boundaries of Al, extending the Read-Shockley's simple dislocation model.
